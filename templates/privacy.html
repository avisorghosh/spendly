Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. When you use the app, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and allowing you to manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request deletion of your account and data, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
